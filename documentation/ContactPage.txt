This document will outline important information regarding the contact page. 

Contact Page structure: 
- Contact.css which includes styling needed for structure these are predefined and can be adjusted
- Contact.jsx which includes the different components including text boxes, buttons, text, and images

Additional important files:
- Footer.css and Footer.jsx for footer styling/modification
- Header.css and Header.jsx for header styling/modification 


Linking contact page with your actual email:
- https://dashboard.emailjs.com/admin 
- Navigate to link above and sign in with the pono pono peace initiative email that you'd like to receive emails at (can be your personal as well)
- Go to email templates
- Click create new templates
- Use the following template (or modify if needed):
        <div style="font-family: system-ui, sans-serif, Arial; font-size: 14px">
          <div>

            <p><strong>First Name:</strong> {{first_name}}</p>
            <p><strong>Last Name:</strong> {{last_name}}</p>
            <p><strong>Email:</strong> {{from_email}}</p>
            <p><strong>Message:</strong></p>
            <p>{{message}}</p>
          </div>
        </div>
- Apply changes (you can go back and edit at any time)

    emailjs
      .send(
